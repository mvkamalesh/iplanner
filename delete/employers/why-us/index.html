Why us

It is the easiest equation. The global leaders in staffing and recruiting coupled with the world’s largest network of candidates. And what do you get? The best talent pool at your finger tips

 

We pride ourselves on being the global leaders of HR and staffing solutions, working with more than 700,000 associates globally. With that comes the responsibility to maintain high standards. High standards of client service and candidate care.

 

We understand that our relationships with our clients and our candidates are cornerstone of our success. This is why our teams are looking to strengthen our services as we strive to build stronger relationships across all facets of our network.

 

However, our ambitions don’t just stop at our services. The Adecco Institute is at the forefront of HR thought leadership and our Candidate International Mobility Programme is the best in the business. Compliance, ethics and corporate social responsibility are more than just buzzwords to us – they are a part of our efforts to provide the best to our clients.

 

This commitment to our clients ad candidates, coupled with our unmatched global presence and local expertise, is how we stay ahead of our competition. So, why work with anybody else? Work with the best in the world. Work with Adecco.
